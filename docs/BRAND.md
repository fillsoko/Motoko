# Brand — motoko

## Wordmark

- Always lowercase: `motoko`
- Typeface: Cormorant Garamond Light (400 weight)
- Never bold, never uppercase, never italicised
- Minimum clear-space: 1em on all sides

## Brand essence

Motoko is serious about motorcycles. The brand communicates craft, confidence, and quiet authority. No hype, no lifestyle fluff. Think editorial motorcycle journalism — Le Mans start energy, not showroom polish.

## Voice

- Direct, minimal, confident
- German precision with international readability
- No exclamation marks in headlines
- No emoji anywhere on the site
- Bilingual consideration: primary German, secondary English for US travellers

## Photography

- Black-and-white only — no colour photography anywhere
- Large-format, editorial composition
- Subject matter: bikes in motion, mechanical detail, road landscapes, rider silhouettes
- Never: stock photography, staged showroom shots, lifestyle/branding clichés
- Placeholder approach: dark grey boxes (`#1A1A1A`) at correct aspect ratios

## Key USP

Daily comprehensive insurance cover for rented bikes — rare in the German market. This must surface clearly and be treated with visual weight, not buried in fine print.

## Partners & integrations

- **RIPE**: external rental/booking platform — all "Book" CTAs link here
- **Komoot**: tour route details — tour cards link to Komoot routes
- Travel agency partnerships for US inbound travellers
